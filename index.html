About Us

BriXnetwork is open source blockchain project. Anyone can participate in network. Works on regular computers and older hardware.

BriXnetwork focuses on simple and practical system. Low transaction costs for all People worldwide. Uses P2P port 8327 for network communication.

Key Features

Simple Design

Works on regular computers and older laptops. Has SegWit for low-cost transactions.

Old Hardware Support

Computers from 2016 can run full nodes and mine blocks. Get rewards in units.

No Ordinals Protocol

Blockchain stays focused on transactions. No extra protocols added.

For Everyone

Open to all People. No corporate control. Equal access for everyone.

op_return Support

Store transaction hashes and confirmations in op_return field.

Network Focus

BriXnetwork simply efficient blockchain. System preserves network clarity and optimal performance for essential transactions. The absence of the Ordinals protocol is a key foundation. Ordinals are digital clutter that unnecessarily burden the blockchain. People can store document confirmations and purchase hashes in the op_return option.

Project Goals

  • Open blockchain for everyone
  • Support independent systems
  • Simple and practical design
  • Low cost transactions
