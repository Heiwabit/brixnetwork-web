About Us

BriXnetwork is open source blockchain project. Anyone can participate in network. Works on regular computers and older hardware.

BriXnetwork focuses on simple and practical system. Low transaction costs for all users worldwide. Uses P2P port 8327 for network communication.

Key Features

Simple Design

Works on regular computers and older laptops. Has SegWit for low-cost transactions.

Old Hardware Support

Computers from 2016 can run full nodes and mine blocks. Get rewards in coins.

No Ordinals Protocol

Blockchain stays focused on transactions. No extra protocols added.

For Everyone

Open to all users. No corporate control. Equal access for everyone.

op_return Support

Store transaction hashes and confirmations in op_return field.

Network Focus

BriXnetwork blockchain focuses on transactions. No Ordinals protocol. Simple and practical system.
